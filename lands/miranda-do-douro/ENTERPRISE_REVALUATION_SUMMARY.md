## Best Strategy Per Spec

### Spec 1 — The Bulk Producer
Grow medicinal herbs at scale (1,000-2,000m²). Sell bulk dried herbs + tinctures wholesale. Foraging tours monetize 5ha terrain. NOT for workshops or retail (remote). **Revenue add: €5,000-15,000/yr.**

### Spec 2 — The Teaching Apothecary
Full medicinal product line processed in existing building. Weekend herbalism workshops (€50-100/person). City = customer base. Building = indoor teaching space. **Revenue add: €12,000-25,000/yr.** Highest % increase.

### Spec 3 — The Bulk Tincturer
500m² medicinal herbs behind stone walls. Bulk tincture production for wholesale. Foraging tours on 1.4ha. Remote = production focus, not retail. **Revenue add: €8,000-18,000/yr.**

### Spec 4 — The Distiller + Retailer
2,000m²+ medicinal herbs. Stream = free distillation cooling. EN218 farm shop sells everything retail. Workshops with stream setting. **Revenue add: €18,000-38,000/yr.** Highest absolute increase.

### Spec 5 — The Streamside Apothecary
200m² medicinal garden. Stream = distillation + workshop ambiance. Well = processing water. City = customers. Outdoor workshops along stream. **Revenue add: €10,000-22,000/yr.**
