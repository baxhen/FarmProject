## PART 7: REGULATORY & FOOD SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS (PORTUGAL)

### Food Business Registration

**Required for ALL value-added food products**:

1. **Register with ASAE** (Autoridade de Segurança Alimentar e Económica)
   - Online registration: Free
   - Declare: Production facility, product types
   - Timeline: 2-4 weeks

2. **Facility Requirements**:
   - Dedicated food preparation area (can be part of home kitchen if clean, organized)
   - Clean water source (your wells - must test water quality)
   - Waste disposal (compost, recycling)
   - Pest control plan (documentation)

3. **HACCP Plan** (Basic):
   - Not required for very small producers (<2 employees)
   - BUT good practice: Document critical control points
   - Example: Dehydrator temperature logs, pasteurization time/temp records

---

### Product-Specific Regulations

#### **Infused Oils/Vinegars**:
- **Risk**: Botulism (if fresh herbs in oil without acidification)
- **Solution**:
  - Dry herbs completely before infusing in oil (no moisture = no botulism)
  - OR acidify (add citric acid, vinegar)
  - OR refrigerate and label "Keep refrigerated, use within 2 weeks"
- **Label**: Ingredients, net weight, best before date, allergens (if any), your contact info

#### **Dried Herbs**:
- Low risk (dry = shelf-stable)
- **Label**: Product name, net weight, best before date (12-24 months), storage instructions

#### **Bottled Juices/Sauces**:
- **Pasteurization required** (unless refrigerated and labeled "perishable")
- **pH testing**: If pH >4.6 = high risk (requires pressure canning OR refrigeration)
  - Most fruit juices: pH 3-4 (safe with pasteurization)
  - Tomato sauces: pH 4-5 (add citric acid to lower pH OR pressure can)
- **Hot-fill method**: Pasteurize, fill bottles hot (85-90°C), seal immediately (creates vacuum)

#### **Herbal Tinctures/Extracts**:
- **Food supplement** (not medicine - cannot make health claims)
- **Alcohol content**: If >1.2% ABV = must comply with alcohol regulations (easier: label as "food flavoring" if <15% alcohol)
- **Label**: "Food supplement" OR "Flavoring extract", ingredients, alcohol %, use instructions
