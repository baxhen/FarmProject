## Why Medicinal Herbs on Spec 3?

1.43ha = room for 500m²+ medicinal herbs. Stone walls = thermal microclimate (south-facing walls store heat — perfect for tender medicinals like holy basil, ashwagandha as annuals). Remote = focus on production, not retail. Bulk wholesale model fits Sendim's lower land cost.

## Recommended Enterprises

### 1. Scale Medicinal Herb Production (500m²)
- 500m² medicinal herbs — more than spec_2 (200m²), less than spec_4 (2,000m²)
- Cold-hardy species: echinacea, valerian, St. John's wort, elderberry, yarrow
- Stone wall microclimate: experiment with holy basil as annual (thermal mass extends season)
- Revenue: €4,000-10,000/yr

### 2. Bulk Tincture Production
- Process harvest into tinctures for wholesale
- Lower labor than retail — make large batches, sell to shops/herbalists
- Revenue: €3,000-8,000/yr

### 3. Foraging Tours
- 1.43ha = oak grove + scrub + open areas
- Combined with stone wall heritage (traditional Sendim farm tourism)
- Revenue: €1,000-3,000/yr

## Not Recommended
- Workshops (remote, no building, 20km from Miranda)
- Distillation (no stream)
- Farm shop (dirt road, no frontage)
