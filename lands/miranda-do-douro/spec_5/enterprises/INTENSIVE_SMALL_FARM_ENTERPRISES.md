## Enterprise 1: Stream-Irrigated Vegetables

**The spec_5 advantage**: Stream = gravity-fed drip irrigation. No pump. No electricity. No water bill. Ever.

**Scale**: 300m² market + 200m² kitchen garden = 500m² total
**Water**: Stream via gravity drip lines. Well as backup.
**Investment**: €300-600 (drip tape, seeds, amendments)
**Revenue Year 1**: €1,200-2,500. **Year 3+**: €3,000-6,000.

**Stream-Irrigation Layout**:
- Beds parallel to stream contour
- Main line along highest stream bank
- Drip tape runs downhill from main line
- Zero energy — gravity does the work
- If stream is below garden level: ram pump (uses stream's own flow to lift water, zero electricity, €200-500)

**Crop selection**: Same as spec_2 — biointensive, succession planting, season extension.

## Enterprise 2: Eggs — Stream-Watered Chickens

**Scale**: 10-15 hens. Stream = drinking water at zero labor (chickens drink from stream edge).
**Investment**: €250-400
**Revenue**: €200-450 surplus + €600-800 self-sufficiency

**Stream advantage for chickens**: No carrying water. Chickens free-range along stream corridor. Insects + vegetation near water = diverse forage. Cooler microclimate near water in summer.
