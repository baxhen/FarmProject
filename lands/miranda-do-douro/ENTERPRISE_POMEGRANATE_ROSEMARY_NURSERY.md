## POMEGRANATE — Why Miranda?

Pomegranates THRIVE in hot dry summers + cold winters. Miranda's climate (Csb, 400-500mm, 800-1,200 chill hours) is ideal. The fruit needs: hot summers for sugar development, cold winters for dormancy (but not below -12°C). Miranda provides both.

**Products from one crop**:
- Fresh fruit: €2-5/kg (farmers market, local shops)
- Arils (fresh seeds): €8-15/kg (restaurants, premium grocers)
- Juice: €5-8/L (bottled, farm shop)
- Dried arils: €15-25/kg (trail mix, baking, export)
- Seedlings/saplings: €8-15/plant (nursery sales)
- Orchard tourism: blossom (May-Jun) + harvest (Oct-Nov)

## ROSEMARY — The Miranda Perfect Crop

Rosemary is the single best climate-matched crop for Miranda. Hot dry summers concentrate essential oils. Cold winters are within tolerance (-15°C). Poor rocky schist soil = ideal stress conditions for maximum oil content.

**Why rosemary over other herbs**:
1. **Climate PERFECTION**: Miranda = rosemary's natural habitat (Mediterranean mountains)
2. **Multiple products**: Fresh bundles, dried culinary, essential oil, hydrosol, infused oil, soap, honey forage, nursery plants
3. **Zero water** once established. Survives on rainfall alone.
4. **15-25 year productive life** from single planting
5. **Fire-resistant**: Strategic firebreak planting around property
6. **Bragança price proof**: Essential oil at €6-9/10ml. Dried herbs at €15-35/kg.
