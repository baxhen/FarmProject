## Enterprise Opportunities — Prioritized

### TIER 1 — Implement Immediately (Year 1-2)

**1. Medicinal Herb Garden + Dried Herb Apothecary** (Score: 4.8/5)
- **Space**: 80-200m² (can start with 7.4m² SHTF garden as template)
- **Species**: Calendula, chamomile, echinacea, lavender, lemon balm, peppermint, rosemary, sage, thyme, yarrow
- **Products**: Dried herbs (Tier 1), herbal tea blends (Tier 1), herbal bath soaks (Tier 1)
- **Revenue**: €3,000-10,000/year from 100m²
- **Startup**: €200-500 (seeds, drying racks, packaging)
- **Regulation**: Tier 1 — agricultural activity registration only
- **Workshop link**: "Grow Your Own Pharmacy" — €25-35/person

**2. Herbal Tea Blending** (Score: 4.5/5)
- **Products**: Sleep blend (chamomile + lavender + lemon balm), Digest blend (peppermint + ginger + fennel), Calm blend (holy basil + lemon balm + rose), Energy blend (rosemary + sage + mint)
- **Margin**: €0.80-1.50 cost, €6-12 retail per 50g tin
- **Startup**: €100-300 (bulk jars, tins, labels, scale)
- **Already have**: Rosemary, thyme, oregano, lavender, sage growing

**3. Foraging/Wildcrafting Walks** (Score: 4.3/5)
- **Miranda species**: Wild thyme, oregano, rosemary (naturalized), oak moss, elderberry, rose hip, nettle, dandelion
- **Perais species**: Holm oak acorns, cork oak moss, wild asparagus, watercress (stream), rockrose (esteva)
- **Format**: 2-3 hour guided walk. Identify + harvest + prepare (tea tasting at end)
- **Revenue**: €20-35/person × 8-12 people = €160-420 per walk
- **Startup**: €0 (no infrastructure — walk existing land)
- **Season**: Spring-Fall. Monthly walks = €1,500-4,000/year

### TIER 2 — Implement Year 2-3 (After Registered Kitchen)

**4. Tincture & Extract Production** (Score: 4.0/5)
- **Products**: Echinacea tincture, chamomile tincture, lemon balm tincture, adaptogen blends
- **Process**: Herb + alcohol (vodka/brandy) + time. 1:4 fresh herb ratio. Macerate 4-6 weeks. Press. Bottle.
- **Equipment**: Mason jars, alcohol, cheesecloth, press, amber bottles — €200-400
- **Margin**: Cost €2-4 per 30ml. Retail €10-20. 400-900% margin.
- **Regulation**: Food supplement or cosmetic? Check ASAEs. May need notification, not full license.

**5. Essential Oil & Hydrosol Distillation** (Score: 3.8/5)
- **Spec_5 stream advantage**: Free cooling water for condenser
- **Crops**: Lavender, rosemary, thyme (high essential oil content)
- **Equipment**: Copper still (€500-1,500). Significant plant material needed (100kg lavender = ~1L essential oil).
- **Products**: Essential oils (€20-50 per 10ml), hydrosols (€8-15 per 100ml)
- **Better as Year 4+**: Requires large herb planting to justify still investment

### TIER 3 — Year 3+ (Registered Kitchen + Established Systems)

**6. Herbal Salves, Balms & Skincare** (Score: 3.5/5)
- Already analyzed in detail in spec_2 VALUE_ADDED_PROCESSING_PLAN (Tier 4 cosmetics)
- Add medicinal herbs as ingredients: calendula salve, arnica balm, comfrey cream
- EU Cosmetics Regulation applies. CPSR needed for each formulation.

**7. Fermented Foods** (Score: 3.3/5)
- Sauerkraut, kimchi, fermented vegetables, kombucha
- High-value probiotic market. Requires HACCP + registered kitchen (Tier 2-3).
- Niche product for farmers market + health food stores
