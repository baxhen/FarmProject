# Site Visit Checklist — Spec 2 (0.6ha, Miranda do Douro)

**Price**: ~€50,000 | **Score**: 80→81/100 | **Agent**: A Minha Casa (AMI 11757, Ref AMC250164 57 000)

---

## 1. Price Negotiation

| Item | Target | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Fair value at 80pts | €45,000-55,000 | Already best-value Miranda property |
| Opening offer | €40,000-45,000 | Justify: building regularization cost, well not confirmed, 0.6ha small |
| Walk-away price | €60,000 | Above this, spec_5 at €30k + €40k to build dwelling = comparable |
| Price/m² comparison | ~€83/m² | Spec_5 = €45/m². Premium justified by building + trees. |

**Negotiation leverage**:
- Building is "agricultural support" — conversion to dwelling costs YOU money
- Fruit trees are 5yr but unquantified — how many, what condition?
- 0.6ha = small. Adjacent land availability unknown.
- Seller: ask why selling. If inherited/relocating = motivated.

---

## 2. Legal Documents

**Before visit**:
- [ ] Caderneta predial — confirms 4,560m² or 6,000m²? (listing discrepancy)
- [ ] Certidão permanente — encumbrances, mortgages
- [ ] Building registration: is it on caderneta? As "construção de apoio agrícola"?

**During/after visit**:
- [ ] **PIP for habitação do agricultor** — CRITICAL. Can agricultural support building be converted to dwelling?
- [ ] Confirm NOT RAN/REN (PDM report says no, but verify with câmara)
- [ ] Verify PDM "Vinhas" designation — does entire parcel qualify?
- [ ] Check building license history: was it built with license? When?
- [ ] Verify septic tank compliance (fossa céptica) — legal? Functional?

**Red flag**: Building NOT on caderneta at all. This means it's illegal — regularization may be impossible or very expensive.

---

## 3. Water Verification

### Public Water
- [ ] **Connection status**: Confirmed active? Meter present? Bill/receipt from last month?
- [ ] **Flow rate**: Turn on tap. Measure L/min. Target: >10 L/min.
- [ ] **Pressure**: Adequate for shower + irrigation simultaneously?
- [ ] **Monthly cost**: Ask for recent water bills. Agricultural or domestic rate?
- [ ] **Summer restrictions**: Does Miranda restrict water in drought? Ask neighbors.

### Irrigation
- [ ] **Drip system**: Public water pressure adequate for 300m² drip irrigation?
- [ ] **Storage tank**: Where could 1,000-2,000L tank go for irrigation buffer?
- [ ] **Rainwater**: Roof area for catchment? m² × 450mm = X liters/year.

---

## 4. Soil Testing

- [ ] **Kitchen garden area ("zona propicia para horta")**: pH, texture, depth. This is confirmed fertile — verify.
- [ ] **Orchard soil**: Test under almond trees. What's the organic layer like?
- [ ] **Drainage**: Flat land can waterlog. Dig test hole, check after rain.
- [ ] **Compaction**: Previous agricultural use? Any hardpan?
- [ ] **Earthworm count**: Under trees + in garden area (should differ)

---

## 5. Existing Resources Inventory

### Building (CRITICAL — this is the spec_2 premium)
- [ ] **Structural**: Walls, roof, floor. Any cracks, damp, mold?
- [ ] **Kitchen**: Fireplace functional? Sink connections — water runs? Drain works?
- [ ] **Bathroom**: "Praticamente concluída" — what's missing? Toilet installed? Shower? Tiles?
- [ ] **Septic**: Locate fossa. Any smell? When last emptied? Compliance certificate?
- [ ] **Electrification**: Is there electricity? If not, nearest pole distance.
- [ ] **Damp/waterproofing**: Flat terrain + building = check for rising damp on walls.
- [ ] **Insulation**: Walls, roof? Winter habitability?
- [ ] **m² of building**: Measure. Is it >30m²? (minimum for dwelling conversion)
- [ ] **Photos**: Every room, ceiling, floor, walls, bathroom, kitchen, septic access, electrical panel

### Fruit Trees
- [ ] **Count by species**: Almond (how many?), apple, cherry, plum, peach, pomegranate
- [ ] **Age verification**: "5 years" per listing. Check trunk diameter (almond 5yr = ~8-12cm)
- [ ] **Health**: Leaf color, fruit set, disease signs, pest damage
- [ ] **Pruning history**: Well-maintained or neglected?
- [ ] **Production estimate**: How many kg/year from almond trees? Ask seller.

### Fencing
- [ ] **"Totalmente vedado"**: Walk perimeter. What material? Height? Condition?
- [ ] **Predator-proof?**: Can foxes/dogs get under/through?
- [ ] **Gates**: Functional? Vehicle access gate?

---

## 6. Infrastructure

- [ ] **Electricity**: If not connected: nearest pole distance. E-Redes quote. If connected: check panel.
- [ ] **Road**: Rural road — condition? Who maintains? Winter access?
- [ ] **Internet**: 4G/5G signal test on phone. Satellite option (Starlink)?
- [ ] **Waste**: Municipal collection on this road? Nearest ecoponto?

---

## 7. Neighbor Interview

- [ ] Water reliability in summer? Ever restricted?
- [ ] Frost dates? (local knowledge for orchard planning)
- [ ] Wildlife pressure? Wild boar in orchard?
- [ ] History of property? Why selling?
- [ ] Community: farmers market days? Local associations?

---

## 8. Red Flags — Spec 2

| Red Flag | Severity | Check |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Building not registered | **CRITICAL** | If not on caderneta, walk away. This is the property's main asset. |
| Septic not compliant | **HIGH** | New septic = €3,000-8,000. Factor into offer. |
| Water not actually connected | **HIGH** | Listing says "água da rede pública" — verify, don't assume. |
| Trees diseased/neglected | MEDIUM | 5yr trees with disease = removal cost, not production asset. |
| Actual area is 4,560m² not 6,000m² | MEDIUM | Caderneta says one thing, listing another. Which is true? |

---

## 9. Visit Logistics

**Best time**: July-August (water stress visible, fruit harvest season — see trees producing). OR February (almond blossom — verify tree health + bee potential).

**Bring**: Soil test kit, measuring tape, camera, water flow test container (10L bucket + timer), notebook.

**Duration**: 2-3 hours. 0.6ha is quick to walk. Building inspection takes longest.
