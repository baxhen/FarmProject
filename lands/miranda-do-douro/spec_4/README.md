The best-connected Miranda property. Stream on-site is unique — no other Miranda property has confirmed surface water. 100m of national road frontage = commercial visibility, farm shop potential. Lameiro (wet meadow) = natural premium pasture. Near city = short supply chains. €125k is expensive per hectare but location + stream + road justify the premium over spec_1 and spec_3.

## Top 3 Strengths
1. **Stream + lameiro**: Only Miranda property with confirmed surface water. Natural irrigation + livestock water + wet meadow pasture.
2. **EN218 frontage (100m)**: National road = visibility, access, farm shop potential. Commercial asset none of the other 4 properties have.
3. **City periphery**: Short supply chains, services nearby, tourism potential. Spec_1 is remote, spec_3 is 20km away. This is at Miranda's edge.

## Top 3 Challenges
1. **Expensive**: €42,590/ha — 2× spec_1 and 2.8× spec_3. Paying for location + stream.
2. **Espaço Florestal**: Forest classification less favorable than agricultural for building. "Possibilidades de construção" needs verification.
3. **No existing building**: Starting from zero on 3ha. EN218 proximity helps (commercial justification).
