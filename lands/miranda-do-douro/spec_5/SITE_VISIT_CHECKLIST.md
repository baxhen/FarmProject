## 3. Water Verification — THE PREMIUM ASSET

### Well
- [ ] **Flow rate test**: CRITICAL. Pump for 1 hour. Measure L/hr. Target: >1,000 L/hr.
- [ ] **Depth**: How deep? (affects pump cost — deeper = more expensive)
- [ ] **Water quality**: Lab test for potability. If potable = drinking water solved.
- [ ] **Pump**: Existing? Age? Working? Submersible or surface?
- [ ] **Wellhead condition**: Sealed? Protected from contamination?
- [ ] **Year-round reliability**: Ask neighbors — does water table drop in summer?
- [ ] **Well license**: APA registration number? Legal?

### Stream
- [ ] **Flow rate**: Summer flow? Measure at narrowest point. L/min?
- [ ] **Year-round?**: CRITICAL. Does stream flow in August? Ask neighbors.
- [ ] **Water quality**: Livestock-safe? Irrigation-safe?
- [ ] **Elevation drop**: For gravity irrigation or ram pump. Measure with phone GPS altimeter.
- [ ] **Stream path**: Walk entire length on property. Map.
- [ ] **Riparian vegetation**: Healthy? Erosion? Shade cover?
- [ ] **Flood evidence**: High water marks on banks? Debris in trees?

**Water verdict**: If well flows >1,000 L/hr AND stream is year-round, this is the best-watered property in Miranda. Score 14→15. If well is dry and stream seasonal, score drops to 9-10.

## 8. Red Flags

| Red Flag | Severity | Check |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Well is dry or <500 L/hr | **CRITICAL** | This is the property's main asset. Test before offer. |
| Stream is seasonal (dry Aug-Sep) | **HIGH** | Still valuable but "year-round water" claim fails. Adjust offer. |
| Well unlicensed | MEDIUM | Licensing cost + delay. Not a deal-breaker but factor in. |
| Stream polluted upstream | MEDIUM | Test water quality. Agricultural runoff? Sewage? |
| Flood risk | MEDIUM | Check flood marks, ask neighbors. Don't build in flood zone. |

## 9. Visit Logistics

**Best time**: **August** — test stream at lowest flow, well at highest demand. OR **March** — stream at highest flow, assess flood risk.

**Bring**: Bucket + timer (well + stream flow), water collection bottles (2-3 samples), soil kit, GPS, camera, rubber boots (stream assessment).

**Duration**: 2-3 hours. 0.67ha is quick. Water testing takes longest.

**After visit**: Well water to lab (potability). Stream water to lab (irrigation suitability). PIP to câmara. Electricity quote from E-Redes.
