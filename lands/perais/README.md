# Perais Region — Vila Velha de Ródão

**District**: Castelo Branco, Central Portugal
**Municipality**: Vila Velha de Ródão
**Climate**: True Mediterranean (Köppen Csa) — hotter summers, milder winters than Miranda

## Why This Region

- **True Mediterranean climate**: 600-800mm rainfall, longer growing season (220-250 days), less frost
- **Tejo River**: Major water body, biodiversity corridor
- **Portas de Ródão**: Monumento Natural — quartzite cliffs ~170m over the Tejo, major tourist attraction
- **Montado ecosystem**: Traditional Portuguese oak savanna — holm oak + cork oak + grazing
- **Reserva Natural do Tejo Internacional**: Protected area, birdwatching (Egyptian vulture, black stork)
- **Very low land prices**: Depopulated interior = land from €0.50-2.00/m²
- **Foz do Enxarrique**: Middle Paleolithic archaeological site of European importance

## Challenges

- **Severe building restrictions**: Much of region is Espaço Natural e Paisagístico or protected areas
- **Depopulation**: Very low population density, limited services
- **Fire risk**: Hot dry summers + forest/scrub vegetation = high fire danger
- **Multiple regulatory authorities**: ICNF, APA/ARH, DGPC may all need to approve interventions
- **Limited agricultural intensity**: Montado is extensive, not intensive. Low yield per hectare.

## Opportunities

- **Cork**: Portugal produces 50% of world cork. Sobreiro (cork oak) harvesting = 9-year cycle, premium product.
- **Montado products**: Acorn-fed pork (porco preto), holm oak honey, wild mushrooms
- **Nature tourism**: Portas de Ródão, Tejo birdwatching, hiking, geological tourism
- **Carbon credits**: Oak forests = carbon sequestration. Emerging revenue stream.
- **Low acquisition cost**: €7,000 for 1ha. Low financial risk, high ecological value.

## Key Distinction

Perais properties are **conservation/agroforestry assets**, not homestead farms. The Espaço Natural e Paisagístico classification means: manage the ecosystem, harvest renewable products (cork, honey, mushrooms), welcome tourists — but do not build dwellings. Complementary to a Miranda homestead, not a replacement.
