# Plant Nursery Module

**Purpose**: Propagating and selling seedlings, saplings, and grafted trees. Circular enterprise — supplies own farm, sells surplus.
**Value**: Highest value/m² of any farm enterprise. €100-300/m² vs vegetables at €5-15/m².

---

## Why a Nursery?

1. **Circular**: Supplies own farm with plants (saves €500-1,500/yr). Sells surplus.
2. **High value/m²**: 50m² generates €1,500-5,000. Same area in vegetables: €250-750.
3. **Skill progression**: Seed → cutting → grafting = increasing value per plant.
4. **Year-round**: Spring = main season. Fall = perennials. Winter = planning + grafting.
5. **Workshop integration**: "Grow Your Own Garden" seedling sale days. Grafting workshops.
6. **Miranda climate fit**: Cold winters = natural stratification for tree seeds. Hot summers = fast growth.

## Key Species for Miranda Climate

### Vegetables (spring starters)
- Tomatoes, peppers, eggplant, brassicas, lettuce, chard, herbs
- Sell at Miranda farmers market + to other farmers

### Herbs (cuttings + seed)
- **Rosemary** (cuttings): Easy to propagate. €3-5/plant. High demand. User priority.
- Lavender: Cuttings or seed. €3-5. Always sells.
- Thyme, oregano, sage: Easy from cuttings. €3-4.
- Mint, lemon balm: Easy from division. €3-4.

### Fruit Trees (seed + graft)
- **Pomegranate**: Cuttings or seed. €8-12/sapling. User priority. High demand.
- Almond: Seed or graft. €10-15/grafted. Miranda's signature crop.
- Fig: Cuttings. €8-12. Fast seller. Everyone wants figs.
- Olive: Grafted. €12-20. Bragança price reference: BIO 8yr at €15/un.
- Mulberry: Cuttings. €8-12. Multipurpose tree.

### Specialty (differentiation)
- Hardy kiwi: Cuttings. €10-15. Almost zero availability in Portugal.
- Honeyberry: Cuttings. €8-12. Zero availability.
- Goji: Cuttings or seed. €6-10.
- Elderberry: Cuttings. €6-8. Medicinal + culinary demand.

### Medicinal Herbs (high-value starts)
- Echinacea, calendula, chamomile, valerian, St. John's Wort
- €3-6/plant. Niche market. Few suppliers in Portugal.

## Propagation Methods by Value

| Method | Skill | Time to Sale | Value/Plant | Best For |
|--------|-------|-------------|------------|----------|
| Seed (vegetables) | Low | 4-8 weeks | €1.50-3 | Tomatoes, peppers, brassicas |
| Seed (herbs) | Low | 6-12 weeks | €3-5 | Chamomile, calendula, echinacea |
| Cuttings (softwood) | Medium | 8-16 weeks | €3-8 | Rosemary, lavender, sage |
| Cuttings (hardwood) | Medium | 12-24 weeks | €5-12 | Fig, pomegranate, mulberry |
| Division | Low | 4-8 weeks | €3-5 | Mint, lemon balm, chives |
| Grafting | High | 12-24 months | €10-25 | Almond, olive, apple, pear |

**Progression path**: Start with seeds + cuttings (Year 1). Add grafting (Year 2-3). Higher skill = higher margin.

## Market Channels

1. **Farmers market**: Most direct. Miranda weekly market. Customers see plants, buy.
2. **Farm gate**: Nursery sales area. Signage on road.
3. **Workshop days**: "Seedling Sale + Planting Workshop." Combine education + sales.
4. **Other farmers**: Bulk vegetable seedlings to local producers.
5. **Online**: Facebook Marketplace, OLX Portugal. "Plantas de Alecrim — Miranda do Douro."
6. **Landscapers/gardeners**: Bulk herb + ornamental contracts.

## Key Species Detail — User Priorities

### Pomegranate (Punica granatum)
- Propagation: Hardwood cuttings (winter), 80-90% success rate. Also seed (variable fruit quality).
- Varieties: Wonderful (commercial standard), Mollar de Elche (soft seed, Spanish), Acco (soft seed, Israeli).
- Time to sale: 12-18 months from cutting to saleable 50-80cm sapling.
- Price: €8-12/sapling. 2yr trees: €15-20.
- Self-supply: Plant 5-10 on own farm. Sell 50-100/year.

### Rosemary (Rosmarinus officinalis)
- Propagation: Softwood cuttings (spring/summer), 90%+ success. Easiest plant to propagate.
- Time to sale: 8-12 weeks from cutting to rooted plug. 16-20 weeks to 15-20cm pot.
- Price: €3-5/plant. 500 plants/year = €1,500-2,500.
- Self-supply: Unlimited cuttings from own mother plants. Plant hedge, orchard understory, medicinal garden.

### Olive (Olea europaea)
- Propagation: Hardwood cuttings (slow) or grafting onto wild olive rootstock (faster, higher value).
- Time to sale: 18-24 months for grafted tree. 6-12 months for rooted cutting.
- Price: Grafted named variety €12-20. Own-root cutting €8-12. Bragança: BIO 8yr at €15.
- Varieties: Cobrançosa, Madural, Verdeal, Salgueira (all available in Bragança).
