## Regulatory Tiers

| Tier | Products | Requirements |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Dried herbs, teas, raw honey | Agricultural registration. No health claims. |
| 1-2 | Tinctures (alcohol-based) | Grey area. Food supplement notification to DGAV. |
| 4 | Salves, balms, essential oils | EU Cosmetics Regulation (EC 1223/2009). CPSR required. |

**Golden rule**: Never make medical claims. Market as "wellness," "traditional," "aromatherapy."
