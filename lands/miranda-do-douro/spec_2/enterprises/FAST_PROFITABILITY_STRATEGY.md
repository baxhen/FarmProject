## Phase 1: First 30 Days — Immediate Revenue

### Day 1-7: Assessment + Harvest

**Existing Fruit Trees** (IMMEDIATE harvest potential):
- Almonds (5yr): Harvest Aug-Sep. If purchasing in summer, harvest comes with the property.
- Apples, cherries, plums, peaches, pomegranates: Various seasons.
- **Value**: €300-800 in fruit (retail equivalent). Sell surplus at Miranda market.
- **Labor**: 10-20 hours harvest + processing. Zero investment.

**Herb & Greens Quick Crop** (Day 1-30):
- Direct-sow fast crops in prepared beds near building:
  - Radishes: 21-30 days. €3-5/bunch. 20m² = €100-200.
  - Salad mix (arugula, lettuce, mizuna): 21-45 days. €8-12/kg. 30m² = €200-400.
  - Cilantro (Brazilian demand): 30-45 days. €8-12/kg. 20m² = €150-300.
- **Investment**: €50-100 (seeds, compost if needed)
- **Revenue**: €200-600 within 30-60 days
- **Labor**: 15-25 hours

**Egg Production** (Day 1-14 setup):
- Buy 6-8 point-of-lay pullets (start laying within 2-4 weeks): €60-100
- Simple mobile coop (DIY from salvaged materials): €50-150
- Existing fencing = free-range ready
- **Revenue**: 4-6 eggs/day = €3-5/day = €90-150/month
- **Self-sufficiency**: Family egg needs met immediately
- **Investment**: €110-250. **Payback**: 2-3 months.

### Day 8-30: Systems Setup

**Compost System** (Day 1-7):
- 3-bin system from pallets: €0-50
- Start composting immediately (kitchen scraps + garden waste + chicken manure)

**Drip Irrigation** (Day 7-14):
- Flat terrain = simple installation. Main line + drip tape.
- Investment: €200-500. Critical for summer production.

**Additional Fast Crops** (Day 14-30):
- Green beans (bush): 50-60 days. €5-8/kg. 30m² = €200-400.
- Zucchini: 45-60 days. €2-4/kg. 20m² = €150-300.
- Swiss chard: 45-60 days (cut-and-come-again). 20m² = €100-200.

**Phase 1 Total**: Investment €400-900. Revenue €500-1,500 + self-sufficiency €500-1,000.

- **Revenue (Year 1, light)**: €200-500. **Year 2+**: €2,000-5,000.

- Cebolinha (Brazilian chives): 10m². Perennial.
- Hot peppers (malagueta, dedo-de-moça): 30m². €18-25/kg.
- **Investment**: €50-100 (seeds)
- **Revenue (Year 1)**: €500-1,500 from Brazilian community in Portugal

### Chicken Scaling

- Scale from 8 to 15 hens: +€80-120 for pullets
- Egg revenue: €150-250/month (8-12 dozen/week)
- Orchard rotation: chickens under fruit trees = pest control + fertilization

**Phase 2 Total**: Additional investment €800-1,700. Projected Year 1 revenue: €1,500-4,000.

## Phase 3: 90 Days - Year 2 — Medium-Term Systems

### Beekeeping (Spring purchase)

- 2 hives: €500-800 (nucs + equipment)
- Placement: Property edge, southeast-facing
- Forage: Existing fruit trees (almond bloom Feb) + herbs + wild vegetation

- Year 1: Hive establishment, minimal harvest. Year 2: 20-40kg honey.
- **Revenue (Year 2+)**: €500-2,000/year

- **Investment**: €650-900. **Revenue (Year 1)**: €1,000-3,000.
