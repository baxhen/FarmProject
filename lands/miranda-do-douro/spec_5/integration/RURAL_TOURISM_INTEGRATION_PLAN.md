**Context**: 0.67ha with stream, near Miranda city, near EN218. No building (yet).

**Date**: 2026-07-09

## The Stream as Tourism Asset

Spec 5's stream is unique among Miranda properties — it's a tourism differentiator.

**Stream-based experiences**:
- **"Ribeiro do Douro"** — named stream experience
- Water sounds = sensory appeal (relaxation, nature connection)
- Wildlife: birds, amphibians, insects (nature observation)
- Cooling microclimate = comfortable in summer (competitive advantage over dry properties)
- Visual: running water, riparian vegetation, photogenic

**EN218 visibility**: 100m frontage (if confirmed — spec_4 has EN218, spec_5 is "próximo a estrada nacional"). Road sign = free advertising to every car passing Miranda.

## Workshop Offerings (No Building Required)

### 1. Herb Harvest & Streamside Drying (3hrs, €25-35/person)
- Harvest herbs from garden
- Wash in stream (traditional method)
- Solar dry on racks near stream
- Each participant: herb bundle + tea blend
- Season: May-September. Max 10 people.

### 2. Stream Ecology & Farm Tour (2hrs, €15-25/person)
- Walk stream corridor, identify riparian plants
- Well demonstration (traditional Miranda water system)
- Stream-to-irrigation system tour (gravity-fed innovation)
- Tasting: stream-cooled herbal tea + farm snacks
- Season: Year-round. Max 15 people.

### 3. Irrigation Design Workshop (3hrs, €30-45/person)
- Gravity-fed drip irrigation design principles
- Ram pump demonstration (if installed)
- Hands-on: build a small drip system
- For: gardeners, small farmers, permaculture enthusiasts
- Season: Spring-Summer. Max 8 people.

### 4. Beekeeping by the Stream (3hrs, €35-50/person)
- Bees + water relationship (stream = ideal bee habitat)
- Hive inspection, honey tasting
- Streamside honey + herb pairing
- Season: April-September. Max 6 people.

### 5. Farm-to-Stream Picnic (3hrs, €35-55/person)
- Harvest vegetables + herbs from garden
- Prepare simple meal together (outdoor cooking)
- Eat at streamside table
- Each participant: recipe cards + herb bundle
- Season: May-October. Max 10 people.
- Note: No kitchen needed (outdoor cooking). HACCP for food service still applies.

## Tourism Timeline

**Year 1** (no building):
- Outdoor workshops only (streamside, under trees)
- Portable toilet (chemical): €100-300
- Shade structure near stream: €200-500
- Farm stand on road: €200-500
- Revenue: €1,000-2,500

**Year 2-3** (tool shed exists):
- Tool shed as base for workshop materials storage
- Covered outdoor workshop area: €500-1,000
- Better toilet (compost): €500-1,000
- Revenue: €3,000-7,000

**Year 4+** (dwelling built):
- Indoor workshop space (dwelling kitchen for Farm-to-Table)
- Proper bathroom for visitors
- Possible overnight (1-2 rooms in dwelling, Alojamento Local)
- Revenue: €8,000-20,000
