# Value-Added Processing Plan — Spec 5

**Key Challenge**: No existing building (unlike spec_2). Processing must be phased alongside dwelling construction.
**Strategy**: Temporary → Permanent. Tier 1 (dried herbs, no kitchen needed) in shed. Tier 2+ (infused, preserves) when farmer dwelling built with processing kitchen.
**Date**: 2026-07-09

---

## The Building Timeline

| Phase | Timeline | Facility | Products Possible |
|-------|----------|----------|-------------------|
| 0 | Year 1 | Outdoor + tool shed | **Dried herbs only** (solar + electric dehydrator in shed) |
| 1 | Year 2-3 | Tool shed upgrade | Dried herbs + tea blends + simple packaging |
| 2 | Year 3-4 | Farmer dwelling built | **All Tier 1-2**: infused oils, vinegars, preserves |
| 3 | Year 5+ | Registered kitchen in dwelling | **Full range**: Tier 1-2-3. Possible Tier 4 (cosmetics). |

**Cost comparison with spec_2**: Spec_2 has existing building → processing starts Year 1. Spec_5 needs dwelling first for Tier 2+ → processing scales with construction timeline. But €20k purchase savings fund the dwelling.

---

## Phase 0: Year 1 — Dried Herbs (Tier 1)

**Legal**: Agricultural activity registration. No kitchen license for dried agricultural products.

**Facility**: Tool shed (10-15m²) + covered outdoor drying area.

**Equipment**:
- Electric dehydrator (Excalibur 9-tray): €400
- Solar drying racks (outdoor, streamside): €0-30
- Vacuum sealer: €200
- Mason jars, kraft bags, labels: €150
- Digital scale: €30
- **Total**: €780-810

**Products**: Dried rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage, lavender bundles, herbal tea blends, dried Brazilian oregano.

**Revenue Year 1**: €500-1,500. **Year 2-3**: €2,000-6,000.

**Stream advantage**: Wash herbs in stream water (free). Solar pre-dry on racks near stream (humidity from stream... actually this could be a problem. Dry away from stream). Use well water for final wash.

---

## Phase 1: Year 2-3 — Tool Shed as Micro-Processing

**Upgrade shed**: Insulate, add stainless steel work table, shelving, better lighting. €500-1,000.

**New products**: Tea blends (mixing dried herbs), herb rubs/seasonings, gift sets.

**Still Tier 1 only** — no wet processing (infused oils need registered kitchen).

**Revenue Year 2-3**: €3,000-8,000.

---

## Phase 2: Year 3-4 — Farmer Dwelling Kitchen

**Build farmer dwelling** (habitação do agricultor): €40,000-80,000.

**Include in dwelling design**:
- Registered kitchen (stainless steel, washable surfaces, handwash sink)
- Separate entrance for commercial use (optional but recommended)
- Storage room for dried herbs + packaging
- Water from well (tested potable)
- Septic system (required for dwelling anyway)

**Now possible**: All Tier 2 products — infused oils, vinegars, honey infusions, preserves, dried fruit.

**Equipment additions**: Ultrasonic infuser (€250), larger packaging setup (€300).

**Revenue Year 3-5**: €5,000-12,000.

---

## Phase 3: Year 5+ — Full Range

With registered kitchen + established orchard + mature herbs + beekeeping:

**Product range**:
- Dried herbs & teas (Tier 1)
- Infused oils, vinegars, honeys (Tier 2)
- Fruit preserves, dried fruit (Tier 2)
- Herb rubs, seasoning blends (Tier 1-2)
- Possible: Soap, balms (Tier 4, if CPSR obtained)

**Revenue Year 5+**: €10,000-20,000.

## Stream-Specific Processing Opportunities

**Unique to spec_5**:

**Water bath canning**: Stream water = unlimited free water for canning bath (large volumes needed). Well water for final product.

**Herb washing**: Stream provides bulk wash water. Well for final rinse.

**Hydrosol distillation** (future, Tier 4): Steam-distill rosemary/lavender for hydrosol + essential oil. Stream water for condenser cooling. This is spec_5's unique processing opportunity — essential oil distillation needs lots of cold water. Stream provides it free.

**Distillation setup**: Copper still (€500-1,500). Stream-fed condenser. Products: rosemary hydrosol, lavender hydrosol, essential oils. High value, zero water cost. Year 4+ opportunity.
