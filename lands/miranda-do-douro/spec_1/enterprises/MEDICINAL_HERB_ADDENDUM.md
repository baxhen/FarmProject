## Why Medicinal Herbs on Spec 1?

5ha = scale advantage. Grow medicinal herbs at volumes other specs can't match. Sell bulk dried herbs + bulk tinctures to wholesalers, herbalists, and processors. NOT for retail/workshops (remote location). Foraging tours monetize 5ha of diverse terrain.

## Recommended Enterprises

### 1. Bulk Medicinal Herb Production (1,000-2,000m²)
- **Species**: Echinacea, valerian, St. John's wort, yarrow, elderberry — all cold-hardy Miranda species
- **Model**: Low-maintenance perennials. Plant once, harvest annually.
- **Processing**: On-site drying (solar + electric). Bulk packaging (1-5kg bags).
- **Buyer**: Portuguese herbalists, tea companies, extract manufacturers
- **Revenue**: €3,000-10,000/yr

### 2. Foraging/Wildcrafting Tours
- **Terrain advantage**: 5ha = oak forest, scrubland, possible watercourses
- **Species**: Wild thyme, oregano, rosemary (naturalized), oak moss, elderberry, rose hip, nettle
- **Format**: 2-3hr guided walk. Monthly April-October.
- **Revenue**: €1,500-3,000/yr
- **Investment**: €0 (no infrastructure)

### 3. Bulk Tincture Production
- **Model**: Process harvest into tinctures for wholesale
- **Equipment**: Large mason jars, alcohol, press. Scale up from spec_2/5 small-batch.
- **Revenue**: €2,000-5,000/yr

## Not Recommended for Spec 1
- Workshops (remote, no building, no city customers)
- Tea blending (retail-focused, needs customer access)
- Distillation (no stream)
