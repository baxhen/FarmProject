# Gap-to-95 Analysis — All 6 Properties

**Question**: What would need to change for each property to score 95+/100? What price makes the current score worthwhile?
**Date**: 2026-07-09
**Method**: Reverse scorecard — identify what drives each point gap, calculate development cost to close gap, determine fair price at current score.

---

## How Scoring Works — The 95 Threshold

A 95/100 property is near-perfect:
- Legal: dwelling confirmed, no protected area conflicts (18-20/20)
- Water: multiple confirmed sources, year-round (14-15/15)
- Soil: deep, fertile, well-drained (13-15/15)
- Climate: ideal for desired enterprises (9-10/10)
- Buildings: legal dwelling exists, habitable (9-10/10)
- Infrastructure: all utilities connected, excellent access (9-10/10)
- Enterprises: diverse, synergistic, profitable (9-10/10)
- Permaculture: excellent design potential, diverse microclimates (5/5)
- Expansion: room to grow, adjacent land available (4-5/5)

**No property evaluated reaches 95.** The highest is spec_2 at 80. The gap from 80 to 95 is 15 points — each point costs money, time, or both.
