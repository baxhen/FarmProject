# Spec 3 — Sendim Property (Miranda do Douro)

**Score**: 72/100 — **BUY**
**Size**: 14,300 m² (1.43 ha)
**Price**: €22,000 (€1.54/m², €15,385/ha)
**Key features**: Stone walls, south slope, Espaço Agrícola, NOT RAN/REN, oak + pine trees, near Sendim

## Executive Summary

Solid 1.43ha agricultural property near Sendim village. Fully enclosed by traditional stone walls — premium infrastructure. Slight south-facing slope, good soil for vegetable garden, established oak and pine trees. Cleaner legal situation than spec_1 (no RAN/REN). Good middle-ground size between spec_2 (0.6ha) and spec_1 (5ha). Best of the Miranda properties for mixed small-scale farming IF water can be confirmed.

## Top 3 Strengths
1. **Stone walls**: Fully demarcated by traditional Portuguese stone walls. Permanent, beautiful, zero-maintenance fencing. Replacement value: €10,000-20,000.
2. **NOT RAN/REN**: Cleaner legal pathway than spec_1. Farmer dwelling possible with agricultural exploitation demonstration.
3. **Versatile size (1.43ha)**: Big enough for small livestock (6-10 sheep/goats) + orchard + vegetables. Small enough to manage part-time.

## Top 3 Challenges
1. **No water source confirmed**: Need well or public water connection. 400-500mm rainfall = irrigation essential.
2. **No existing building**: Starting from zero. Farmer dwelling pathway exists but requires investment.
3. **Price unknown**: Same agent as spec_2. Estimate €15,000-35,000 for 1.43ha in Sendim area.
