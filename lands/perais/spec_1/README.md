# Spec 1 — Perais Property

**Score**: 48/100 — **PROBABLY NOT** (as homestead/farm)
**Alternative**: Conservation/agroforestry asset — **BUY at €7,000** with correct expectations
**Size**: 10,460 m² (1.046 ha)
**Price**: €7,000 (€0.67/m²)
**Key features**: Flat, south-facing, holm oak + cork oak grove, almost entirely fenced, good access

## Executive Summary

Beautiful 1ha of traditional Portuguese montado (oak savanna) near Vale de Pousadas. Flat, fenced, south-facing. But classified as Espaço Natural e Paisagístico — building essentially impossible. Cannot be a homestead. Valuable as: cork harvesting, beekeeping site, nature tourism base, carbon credit asset, or part of multi-property strategy where you live elsewhere and harvest from here.

## Top 3 Strengths
1. **€7,000 price**: Extremely low financial risk. €0.67/m² is near the floor for accessible land.
2. **Montado ecosystem**: Mature holm oaks + cork oaks = renewable cork income + honey + mushrooms.
3. **True Mediterranean climate**: 600-800mm rain, milder winters, longer growing season than Miranda.

## Top 3 Challenges
1. **Building essentially impossible**: Espaço Natural e Paisagístico + protected area overlaps. No dwelling.
2. **Multiple regulatory bodies**: ICNF, APA/ARH, DGPC all involved for any intervention.
3. **Small for montado**: 1ha is tiny for traditional cork/acorn economy (typically 10-100ha+).

## Recommendation

**NOT RECOMMENDED as a homestead/farm** (48/100). The Espaço Natural e Paisagístico classification is a near-total blocker for dwelling and building.

**RECOMMENDED as a conservation/agroforestry asset at €7,000** IF:
1. You already have a dwelling elsewhere (e.g., Miranda spec_2)
2. You understand this is a cork/honey/mushroom/tourism site, not a home
3. You accept the regulatory complexity (ICNF, APA, DGPC)
4. You value the ecological asset (mature oaks, biodiversity, carbon)
5. €7,000 is acceptable risk for a long-term, low-maintenance asset

**Multi-Property Strategy**: Live at Miranda spec_2 (0.6ha, intensive production, near city). Harvest cork + honey + mushrooms from Perais (1ha, extensive, low-maintenance). Two complementary properties at different ends of the intensity spectrum.
