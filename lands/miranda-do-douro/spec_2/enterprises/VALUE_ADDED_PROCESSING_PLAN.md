### Phase 3: Cosmetic Products (Tier 4 — Future Option)

**Trigger**: €10,000+ in food product sales. Dedicated processing space. Regulatory confidence.

**Products**:
- Rosemary + honey soap (cold process)
- Lavender hand balm
- Almond oil (from farm almonds — press required)
- Beeswax lip balm

**Legal** (EU Cosmetics Regulation EC 1223/2009):
- CPSR (Cosmetic Product Safety Report): €200-500 per formulation
- Responsible Person (you, EU-based)
- CPNP notification before sale
- GMP-compliant documentation
- Separate from food processing (cross-contamination)
- INCI labeling mandatory

**Equipment**: Soap molds, scale, stick blender, safety gear, curing racks. €300-500.

**Investment**: €800-1,500 (equipment + first CPSR)
**Revenue (Year 4+)**: €5,000-15,000

**Pricing**:
| Product | Cost/Unit | Retail | Margin |
|---------|----------|--------|--------|
| Rosemary-honey soap (100g) | €1.70 | €7-10 | 76-83% |
| Lavender balm (30ml) | €1.50 | €8-12 | 81-88% |
| Beeswax lip balm | €0.50 | €4-6 | 88-92% |

**Note**: Cosmetics = different regulatory path than food. Higher barrier, higher margin. Start with food products (Tier 1-2), add cosmetics only when food business is stable and regulatory process is understood.

## Regulatory Pathway — Step by Step

### Step 1: Agricultural Activity Registration
- Register as agricultural producer (if not already)
- This covers Tier 1 products (dried herbs, raw honey, fresh eggs)
- Contact: Direção Regional de Agricultura (DRAP Norte)

### Step 2: Registered Kitchen (for Tier 2)
- Verify building can be registered as processing kitchen
- Requirements: Washable surfaces, potable water, handwashing, waste disposal, pest control
- Existing building already has: water, sink, septic, separate rooms
- May need: stainless steel surfaces, fly screens, separate handwash sink
- Contact: ASAEs (Autoridade de Segurança Alimentar e Económica)

### Step 3: HACCP Plan (simplified)
- Document processes for each product
- Identify critical control points
- Small producer simplified HACCP available
- Template available from ASAEs

### Step 4: Labeling Compliance
- Product name, ingredients (descending weight), net weight
- Best-before date, storage conditions
- Producer name + address
- Lot number for traceability

### Step 5: Cosmetic Notification (Tier 4 only, future)
- Commission CPSR from qualified safety assessor
- Register on CPNP portal
- Implement GMP documentation
- Separate cosmetic processing from food
