## WAVE 1: STORAGE REVOLUTION (Critical Foundation)

**Theme**: Storage crops + root cellar — the single biggest strategic gap
**Effort**: 3-5 hours
**Impact**: Fundamental strategy shift from "fresh vegetables" to "storage crops as backbone"

### Why This First

Every analysis that touched food production was transformed by the storage crop insight:
- Year 1 budget: 270m² storage crops could provide 575-790kg food vs 300m² fresh vegetables providing seasonal only
- Labor: 50-80 hours/year for storage vs 200-400 for equivalent fresh
- Infrastructure: €1,500 root cellar stores €1,500-2,500/year food, zero energy
- Enterprise strategy: Storage crops sell at 1.5-2× harvest price in winter

This changes the entire farm model. Everything else builds on it.

- [ ] Add sub-enterprises: potatoes, carrots, onions, garlic, squash, beets, cabbage, dried beans
- [ ] Add storage crop portfolio design section

- **Files**: `.claude/skills/enterprise-planner.md`
