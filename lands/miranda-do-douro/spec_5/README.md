**Score**: 77/100 — **BUY**

## Executive Summary

The best-watered property of all 6 evaluated. Well (domestic water) + stream crossing the property (irrigation + livestock). Espaço Agrícola classification with no RAN/REN constraints. Near Miranda city and national road. €30k is fair — you're getting spec_2's location and spec_3's legal clarity with water that beats both.

## Top 3 Strengths
1. **Well + stream**: No other property has both. Well for house/drinking. Stream for irrigation/livestock. Water security solved on day one.
2. **Espaço Agrícola + NOT RAN/REN**: Cleanest legal classification. Farmer dwelling pathway clear.
3. **€30,000 price**: 60% of spec_2's price for similar size and better water. Trade-off: no existing building.

## Top 3 Challenges
1. **No building**: Starting from zero. €40,000-80,000 for farmer dwelling construction.
2. **Small (0.67ha)**: Same size constraint as spec_2. Must focus on high-value/m² enterprises.
3. **Well unquantified**: Flow rate, depth, water quality all unknown. Test before purchase.
